WHO I AM

Hi, I am Konstantinos, an experienced Software Developer with a good background of working in the computer software industry. Specialized in Android Development with Java and Kotlin.

I was born and raised in Greece.
My interest in programming started at early teenage years when I first met the basic concepts of the subject at school and especially on mobile developing when I started playing around with Android's first beta release in 2008.

My career as a Software Developer started soon after my internship. Up to this point I have learned to follow every step of the development process such as estimation, analysis, code plan, programming, unit testing, code review and documentation and at the same time to work with and maintain legacy code.
